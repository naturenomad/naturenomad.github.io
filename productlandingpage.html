Save Earth

How? About News

Life on Earth is in catastrophic decline and our survival depends on stopping it.

This problem is not just for politicians. Every single one of us can change our lives to give our children a life.

It's not easy, to turn the tide. It is huge. It might take some time. It will definitely take getting used to. But the time to start is Right Now.

Be The Change

  • Buy organic - no pesticides and artificial fertilisers - stopping the insect die-off, and so everything else can survive
  • Buy local - remove carbon emissions from transporting what we need
  • Grow our own food - even better
  • Permaculture (ecological) farming - better even again
  • Eat mainly plants
  • Rewild our gardens - plant local, native plants for local, native wildlife
  • Know where the products we buy come from and vote with our money
  • Buy things made of natural materials and vote with our money
  • Buy only the stuff we truly need
  • Focus on time with the people we love instead of -stuff-
  • Stop buying plastic
  • Vote for politicians acting for nature and for the future
  • Choose to live in a small, energy-efficient home
  • Walk, bike, train, sail - remove all the carbon emissions we can from our lives
  • Invest in the green economy, divest from the carbon economy
  • Turn off our smart phone, smart tv, tablet, laptop and spend our time on the people in front of us. The internet harms the climate as much as the airline industry
  • Support rewilding projects and wilderness preservation projects - volunteer and donate

Save Earth supports the following projects

  • Rewilding
  • Forest purchase and protection
  • Coral reef preservation
  • International education on the global biodiversity crisis
  • Campaigning for indigenous hunter-gatherers land rights
  • Campaigning on fisheries and forest destruction

Please sign up here if you would like to learn more or contribute economically to one or more of these efforts.
